Questions worth answering

FAQ

The five we get most often. If yours isn't here, ask on the discovery call — that's what it's for.

Do I have to use Monday.com? +

No. Monday is what we recommend most often because it scales without losing legibility, but the strategy adapts to your stack. We've shipped against HubSpot, Airtable, n8n, Notion, and bespoke Postgres setups. The diagnostic in Phase 1 tells us which.

What is the deposit actually paying for? +

Research, design, the build of your subdomain strategy site, and standing up Monday or n8n if you don't already have it. By the time we ask for the second half, you've walked through the whole strategy and seen the first workflow running.

How is this different from a Zapier consultant? +

A consultant hands you a deck and an invoice. We hand you a hosted site that holds the strategy, the diagrams, and the running workflows behind one URL you can show anyone. Six months later it's still your reference manual, not a file in someone's downloads folder.

Who owns the work after delivery? +

You do. Every credential is vaulted in your Passwordn account. Every workflow runs in your accounts on your billing. The subdomain stays yours as long as you want it.

How fast can you start? +

Discovery call within 48 hours of a signed audit. Strategy site live within 5 business days of the deposit. We hold one Phase 1 slot per week so we don't drop quality.
